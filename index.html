Open software

Free open source software plays enabling role in medical image computing research. We are developing open source imaging informatics tools to support research workflows of the NCI Quantitative Imaging Network.

Open standards

One of our goals is to implement and improve support for interoperable exchange of derived image data using Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine (DICOM) standard.

Open science

We believe open and reproducible science will be enabled by helping researchers to store analysis results in interoperable formats, share them with the community and evaluate novel methods on common datasets.
